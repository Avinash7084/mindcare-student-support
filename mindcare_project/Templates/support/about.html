About MindCare

A dedicated Student Mental Health Support System built to prioritize well-being alongside academic excellence.

Why MindCare?

Academic pressure, irregular sleep patterns, and social isolation are rising challenges among modern students. Often, students hesitate to seek help due to social stigma or a lack of immediate resources.

MindCare was developed to bridge this gap. It serves as a secure, private, and stigma-free digital environment where students can anonymously analyze their psychological well-being and receive immediate guidance.

Our Core Mission

To empower students with interactive, accessible technology that fosters self-awareness, reduces academic stress, and guides them toward healthier mental patterns.

System Features

Mental Assessment

A 15-question scientifically structured quiz tracking stress, anxiety, and sleep health with percentage-based indicators.

Human-like AI Chatbot

An NLP-driven conversational agent that recognizes academic stress triggers and answers with real-time support mechanisms.

Wellness Repository

Curated guides and actionable steps covering sleep hygiene, the Pomodoro technique, and emotional resilience.
